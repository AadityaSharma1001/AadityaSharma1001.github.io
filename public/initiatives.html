Empowering Entrepreneurship at Our College

Welcome to E-Cell

At the E-Cell of IIT Jodhpur, we believe that entrepreneurship is not just about starting companies; it's about solving problems, creating value, and making a difference in the world. Our initiatives are designed to nurture the entrepreneurial spirit, providing students with the resources, mentorship, and opportunities they need to succeed in their ventures.

Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur, a tech enthusiast, or someone looking to make a meaningful impact, there's a place for you here. Explore our initiatives and discover how we can help you turn your ideas into reality.

Startups, Corporates and Funding (SCF)

Our SCF vertical bridges the gap between students and the corporate world by bringing in funding opportunities, corporate partnerships, and government associations. We strive to build a sustainable ecosystem where innovation thrives.

Institute Innovation Council (IIC) and Rankings

IIC drives innovation and excellence in the institute by promoting a culture of creativity, problem-solving, and critical thinking. Our rankings reflect the impact of our initiatives in fostering entrepreneurship and innovation.

Technical Innovation & Startups Council (TISC)

Oversee the incubation center activities at IIT Jodhpur, assist students with startup funding, and manage a network of contacts in the corporate and startup sectors. Act as the E-Cell’s front face by connecting startups with mentorship and organizing pitching events in collaboration with the Incubation Center and investors. Handle collaborations with investors and entrepreneurs, and secure sponsorships for events.

Events, Media and Coverage

Organize and conduct events, workshops, and seminars to enhance entrepreneurship skills among IIT Jodhpur students and boost E-Cell’s brand both on and off campus. These events aim to raise awareness, provide knowledge to aspiring entrepreneurs, and support current startups. We will host an annual inter-college event to foster entrepreneurial culture in Jodhpur. The department will also manage E-Cell’s social media, sharing quality content such as articles, interviews, and updates on E-Cell activities.

Web Development

The Web Development team creates and maintains the online presence of E-Cell. From the official website to event pages, we ensure everything is accessible, visually appealing, and functional for our audience.

Ready to Make an Impact?

If you're passionate about innovation and entrepreneurship, now is the time to get involved. Join us in our mission to empower the next generation of entrepreneurs and innovators. Whether you're looking to start your own venture or support others in theirs, E-Cell has the resources and network you need.
